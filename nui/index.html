Vehicle Key System Help

Commands

  • /getkeys - Get the keys for the vehicle you are currently in.
  • /givekeys [Player ID] - Give your current vehicle keys to another player.
  • /lockpick - Attempt to lockpick the nearest vehicle.

Automatic Features

  • Drive Lock: Doors automatically lock when you start driving.
  • Exit Unlock: Doors automatically unlock when you exit the vehicle.
  • Walk Lock: Doors automatically lock when you walk a certain distance away.

Keybinds

  • [E] - Manually lock or unlock your vehicle when you are on foot.
